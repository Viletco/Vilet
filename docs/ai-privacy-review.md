# AI privacy review

This checklist is not legal advice. Vilét AI remains disabled until reviewed.

- Confirm the approved provider/model, processing location, subprocessors, training controls, abuse monitoring, and retention terms.
- Confirm lawful purpose and accurate notice for chat messages and public website URLs.
- Approve the sensitive-information warning and website ownership/authorization wording.
- Decide whether explicit one-time `sessionStorage` Contact handoff is acceptable.
- Confirm no default transcript persistence, analytics, raw prompt logging, or insights collection.
- Confirm hashed connection-key retention and Upstash expiration behavior.
- Confirm temporary fetched HTML handling and immediate disposal.
- Decide whether a processor agreement, Terms update, consent control, or jurisdiction-specific language is required.
- Record an incident/disable owner and deletion/escalation procedure.

Privacy remains `noindex`; the public notice now describes the disabled state and the proposed behavior if activated.
