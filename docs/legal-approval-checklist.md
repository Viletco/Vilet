# Legal approval checklist

This is an operational review list, not legal advice.

- [ ] Authorized reviewer has read the complete public privacy page.
- [ ] Contact data collected, purpose, retention language, recipients/processors, and user rights accurately reflect real operations.
- [ ] Named contact method is real and approved; no placeholder address is published.
- [ ] Resend and Upstash disclosures are included if those services are activated.
- [ ] Analytics/cookies are absent unless separately approved and disclosed.
- [ ] Reviewer decides whether Terms, cookie notice, consent controls, or jurisdiction-specific disclosures are required.
- [ ] Any required legal page is supplied or approved by qualified counsel before implementation.
- [ ] Approval is recorded with reviewer and date in the owner worksheet/decision registry without confidential advice.

The current site must not invent a legal contact, company registration, address, certification, or policy date.
